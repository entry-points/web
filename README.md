# Entry Points: Pathways and Possibilities to Support Student Learning about Data and AI

A three-part virtual webinar series hosted by the University of Tennessee, Knoxville College of Education, Health, and Human Sciences (CEHHS), highlighting innovative approaches to data science and AI education across K-12 and higher education.

## About the Series

This webinar series brings together national leaders, researchers, and practitioners to explore pathways for expanding data science and AI learning opportunities. Each event focuses on a different educational level and context, creating a comprehensive picture of the current landscape and future possibilities.

### Series Events

**Event 1: National and State Efforts** (February 2026)  
Exploring national frameworks and state-level initiatives for advancing data science and AI education.

**Event 2: Postsecondary Efforts** (March 2026)  
A "Lessons from the Field" event showcasing university programs and degree pathways in data science and AI.

**Event 3: K-12 Efforts** (April 2026)  
Highlighting opportunities to support data science and AI education in classrooms, schools, and districts.

## Series Goals

- Map the current terrain of national initiatives
- Share practical guidance for educators, administrators, and policymakers
- Build networks and collaborations among institutions advancing data and AI learning
